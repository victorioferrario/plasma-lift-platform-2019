How does plasma cosmetology work?

The principle of plasma cosmetology is based on the use of the 4th state of the substance – plasma. It uses plasma arch that occurs when nitrogen and high-frequency energy meet in the air without directly touching the skin, about 1-2mm from the skin. The tissue is treated “sublimate” due to narrowed cold plasma effect.

This way we avoid spreading unwanted heat to the surrounding areas and preserve the healthy tissue. The essence of the treatment is to create a controlled micro-ablation within the top layer of the skin EPIDERMIS.
